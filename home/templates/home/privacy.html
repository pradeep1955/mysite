What happens to your information.

Plain language, no legal filler. Last updated: {% now "F Y" %}.

What this site collects

TinkerStack collects only what's needed to run the site and respond to you directly:

  • Preorder / interest forms — name, email, phone number (optional), and any message you write, when you express interest in a kit.
  • Chat assistant — the questions you type into the chat widget are logged so I can improve the answers over time. Don't type personal information into it; it isn't needed to get an answer.
  • Basic technical data — standard session cookies used to keep the site working (e.g. keeping you logged in if you're a registered author), and server logs that any website generates.

This site does not process payments, and no card or bank details are ever collected here — preorders are followed up manually by email, WhatsApp, or UPI, outside this website.

Why it's collected

Solely to respond to your enquiry, follow up on a preorder, and improve the site and the chat assistant's answers. Nothing here is sold, and nothing is used for purposes beyond what's described on this page.

Third-party services

A few external services are used to run this site, and they may see basic technical information (like your IP address) as a normal part of how the web works:

  • Google Fonts — used for the site's typography.
  • Bootstrap and Font Awesome CDNs — used for site styling and icons.
  • PythonAnywhere — this site's hosting provider.

None of these are given access to the personal information you submit through forms on this site — they're only involved in loading and displaying the page itself.

Affiliate links

Some product links on this site — including in the store and the chat assistant's responses — are affiliate links (for example, to Amazon or Robu.in). If you click one and make a purchase, I may earn a small commission at no extra cost to you. Once you leave this site and land on a retailer's page, that retailer's own privacy policy applies — this policy only covers TinkerStack itself.

How long information is kept

Preorder and interest submissions are kept until they're followed up on and are no longer needed. You can request deletion at any time — see contact details below.

Your choices

You can:

  • Ask what information is held about you
  • Ask for it to be corrected or deleted
  • Simply not submit a form — nothing on this site requires personal information to browse, read, or use the chat assistant for general questions

Children's privacy

This site isn't directed at children, and personal information isn't knowingly collected from anyone under 18.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the "last updated" date at the top of this page will change too. Significant changes will be reflected here directly — there's no separate mailing list for policy updates.

Contact

Questions about your data, or want something corrected or removed? Reach out directly at prdpchowdhary@gmail.comIL.
