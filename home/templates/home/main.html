The balcony that watered itself

ESP32 DS3231 RTC Drip irrigation Arduino

It started with a row of flower pots on the balcony and a familiar problem: whenever a work trip or a busy week got in the way, the plants paid for it.

The fix became a small, reliable build around an ESP32 and a real-time clock so watering could happen on a schedule without drift.

The interesting part wasn't the watering — it was building something that quietly kept a promise every single day without me thinking about it.

That same pattern drives the projects and experiments shared here: simple tools, careful engineering, and useful automation.
